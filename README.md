# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis

## Overview
End-to-end analysis of 2,823 sales transactions to identify 
key revenue drivers across countries, products, and customer segments.

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which month and year had the highest sales?
2. Which country generates the most revenue?
3. Which product line sells the most?
4. Who are the top 10 customers by total sales?
5. What is the relationship between deal size and revenue?

## Tools Used
- Python (pandas) — data cleaning
- SQL (SQLite) — business analysis
- Matplotlib & Seaborn — visualization

## Key Findings
- 2004 was the peak sales year
- USA is the #1 revenue country
- Classic Cars is the top product line
- Medium deals drive the most total revenue
